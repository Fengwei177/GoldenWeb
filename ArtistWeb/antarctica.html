Flag of Antarctica




This is a small project to design a flag for my hometown Dalian. A city in north east China. Dalian sits on a peninsula and is a major port city. Dalian is well-known for its tourism because the plentiful sea produce and beautiful ocean view.

Blue and white field with a red star at the center as the charge. Blue ocean wave represent Dalian as a major port city. The area of white represent the Dalian peninsula surrounded by sea. The red star represent the city and its people.
