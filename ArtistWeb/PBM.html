Project Blue Marble

120 Designs, 3 Years, 1 Flag

Project Blue Marble is an endeavor to design a flag for planet earth. The challenge of this project is to push the limit of design, the eventual goal is to create a flag with simple visuals yet powerful symbols that best defines Earth as a planet and a home.

The project is divided into six sessions throughout three years. Each session will produce twenty designs, building up to 120 total designs at the end of the project. In each session, one design is chosen to be the best flag design for that session, summing up to six best designs at the projectâ€™s end. From those six designs, one is chosen to be final design to conclude the project. Each session is each semester from sophomore to senior year during my career in SAIC.
