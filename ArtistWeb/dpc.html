DPC Tour Poster





This is a project to design a poster for a ficitonal tour by the Chicago indie rock band Driftless Pony Club (DPC). The tour name is The Return of the Rock Sock. The poster functions well to inform the band’s name, tour name, tour dates and locations, and a QR code to the band’s website. The direction for the poster design is to photograph a box that represents DPC’s tour mailing to their fans. The box is heavily damaged because the journey was diffcult, but DPC preseveres and deliever the goods. It will carry on the spirit of Driftless Pony Club to deliever the infinite joy of rock music.
