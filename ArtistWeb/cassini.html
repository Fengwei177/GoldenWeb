Cassini-Huygens





The Cassini probe is the first to orbit the Saturn system. It spent 13 years diving between the rings. It made extremely close observations of Saturn and its moons and taken stunning images. The Huygens probe Cassini carried made the first landing on the outer solar system: on Titan, one of Saturn's moons. Cassini ended its journey by plunging into Saturn's atmosphere and disintegrated in an spectacular glow.
