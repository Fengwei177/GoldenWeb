Flag of Flag Making




The illustration represents a hammer hitting on a grommet. Grommets are metal rings hammered on to a flag to create holes for hooks to catch on and connect the flag to a pole. Red, blue, and white used on this flag are the three most used colors on national flags, and the most used color combination. The two white stripes on the upper hoist and lower fly side of the flag represent double stitching used on most flags to increase durability.
