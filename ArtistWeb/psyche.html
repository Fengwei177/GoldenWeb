16 Psyche





The 16 Psyche asteriod is a metal world. Made out of 90 percent iron and having an magnetic field, this asteriod is thought to be the core of a planet. The theory is that during the planet's formation, it was strike by another body causing the planet to lose its outer layer leaving only the core. This future mission will send a probe to orbit 16 Psyche and determine the validity of this theory.
